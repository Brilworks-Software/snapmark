# 💰 SnapMark Revenue Model & Monetization Strategy

As a high-performance visual communication tool, SnapMark has several viable paths to profitability. Below is a suggested roadmap to transition from a free utility to a sustainable revenue-generating product.

---

## 1. The Freemium Model (Tiered SaaS)

This is the most standard and effective model for browser extensions.

### **Starter ($0 - Free Forever)**
*   **Target**: Casual users, students, individual developers.
*   **Features**:
    *   Region & Visible Area capture.
    *   Basic Annotations (Arrows, Shapes, Text).
    *   Local History (last 10 items).
    *   Standard Blur/Pixelation.

### **Pro ($4.99/mo or $49/year)**
*   **Target**: Professional QA testers, PMs, Power users.
*   **Features**:
    *   **Full Page Scrolling Capture** (The "Killer" Pro feature).
    *   **Unlimited Cloud Storage** (Hosted on Supabase).
    *   **Video Screen Recording** (Future expansion).
    *   **Custom Branding**: Remove "Sent via SnapMark" from shared links.
    *   **Priority Support**.

---

## 2. B2B / Team Collaboration (SaaS)
**Pricing: $12 per user / month**

*   **Shared Team Workspace**: A central dashboard where all team members' screenshots are automatically organized.
*   **Project Folders**: Group screenshots by project/sprint.
*   **Comment & Mention**: Leave feedback directly on a shared screenshot link.
*   **SSO & Security**: Integration with Okta/Azure AD for enterprise security.

---

## 3. White-labeling & Custom Domains
**Pricing: $19/mo (Add-on)**

*   **Custom Domains**: Instead of `snapmark.com/abcd`, users can use `screenshots.yourcompany.com`.
*   **Custom Logo**: Place the company's logo on the screenshot viewer page.
*   **Value**: Excellent for agencies and consultants who want to look professional when sending feedback to clients.

---

## 4. AI-Powered Value Adds (The "AI" Tier)
**Pricing: $29/mo or Credits-based**

*   **AI OCR**: Automatically extract text from a screenshot.
*   **AI Translator**: Translate the text inside an image instantly.
*   **Smart Redaction**: AI automatically detects and blurs PII (Emails, Credit Cards, Names) without manual effort.
*   **Visual Diff**: Compare two screenshots and highlight what changed in the UI.

---

## 5. The "Growth" Strategy (Inbound Marketing)

*   **Viral Loop**: Every free shared link is a landing page for new users. Adding a "Powered by SnapMark" button on every public viewer page drives organic growth.
*   **API for Developers**: Charge developers to use the SnapMark engine to generate automated screenshots for their own apps.
