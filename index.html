Frequently Asked Questions

Is it really free?

Yes. SnapMark is free for individuals. No hidden costs or annoying "Pro" popups for core features.

Where are images stored?

When you share a link, images are stored in your own Supabase project or our secure default bucket. You have full control.

Does it track my data?

Never. We don't use cookies, trackers, or analytics. Your screenshots stay on your machine unless you choose to share them.

Can I use keyboard shortcuts?

Absolutely. Press 'R' for region, 'V' for visible area, and use shortcuts within the editor for lightning-fast markup.
